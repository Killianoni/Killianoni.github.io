Nichify - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-18

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Nichify ("the Service") handles information when you use our content automation tool.

2. Information We Collect

Nichify collects only the minimum information required to operate:

  • OAuth tokens for TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram: stored locally to publish on your behalf. Never shared.
  • Telegram chat ID: used to send approval prompts and receive your accept/refuse decisions.
  • Content you generate: video files, captions, and feedback motifs, stored locally on the machine running Nichify.
  • Publication status: returned by third-party platforms when you publish.

3. How We Use Information

All data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Generate and publish short-form videos to your own accounts at your explicit direction.
  • Send approval prompts to your Telegram and record your decisions.
  • Learn from your refusal motifs to improve future iterations (stored locally only).

4. Data Storage

All personal data and OAuth tokens are stored locally on the machine running the Nichify agent (or in an isolated cloud sandbox you control). No data is transmitted to third parties other than the social media platforms you explicitly connect.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with any third party. Content is only transmitted to the specific platforms you have connected (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) when you approve a publication.

6. Third-Party APIs

Nichify uses the following third-party APIs. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta Privacy Policy
  • Telegram Privacy Policy

7. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained until you revoke them in the respective platform's developer console or delete them from your local configuration. Published content metadata may be retained indefinitely in your local logs unless manually purged.

8. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke Nichify's access tokens through each platform's "Connected Apps" settings.
  • Delete local Nichify data by removing the project folder.
  • Request information about data we hold by emailing the contact below.

9. Children's Privacy

Nichify is not directed to children under 13. If you are under the age required by your jurisdiction's applicable laws, do not use the Service.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated through updates to this page.
